A Renaissance Technologist on a Mission


My Strategic Journey into Cybersecurity

Blending infrastructure and web development with Purpose-Driven Innovation

Introduction

I’m a cybersecurity professional who believes that protecting digital infrastructure goes beyond technical skills—it requires vision, resilience, and the ability to adapt. My career transition into cybersecurity was a calculated decision, driven by a desire to make an impact in an industry where the stakes are high and the work truly matters.

From Boxing to Cybersecurity

For years, I led and coached individuals through challenges that tested mental toughness and strategic thinking. As a boxing coach, I wasn’t just teaching technique—I was helping people overcome mental obstacles, build discipline, and push past their limits. That experience taught me a critical lesson: success in any field depends on mindset, resilience, and a relentless commitment to improvement.

Cybersecurity captured my attention because it demands those same qualities. The transition wasn’t just about learning new skills—it was about applying hard-earned lessons to a domain where critical thinking, vigilance, and strategic action protect what matters most.

Certifications and Achievements

My approach to cybersecurity has been methodical and focused. To ensure I built a strong foundation, I earned the following certifications:

CompTIA A+
CompTIA Network+
CompTIA Security+
Microsoft Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900)
Google Cybersecurity Professional Certification
(Currently Pursuing) CCNA Certification
(Currently Pursuing) BTL1 - SOC Analyst Certification


Beyond certifications, I’m pursuing a B.S. in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance at WGU, now more than halfway complete. I’ve also completed extensive lab work, built a comprehensive digital reference library, and immersed myself in industry-relevant projects.

Documenting the Process: YouTube and Renaissance Technologist

My career transition is more than a personal journey—it’s a blueprint for others who want to pivot into cybersecurity. Through my YouTube channel, Renaissance Technologist, I document every step of my path, from certification prep to hands-on projects.

The channel serves two purposes:

  1. Showcasing Skill Acquisition: Demonstrating real-world skills, problem-solving abilities, and continuous improvement in cybersecurity.
  2. Inspiring Career Changers: Offering guidance, encouragement, and practical advice to those who, like me, are forging new paths later in life.

By sharing my experiences, I aim to normalize career reinvention and show that breaking into tech is achievable with the right mindset and approach.

Vision for the Future: Renaissance Technologist & The John Connor Project

Renaissance Technologist represents more than a YouTube channel—it’s a philosophy. It’s about blending technical mastery with broader personal growth, embracing the challenges that foster subconscious development, and embodying the renaissance ideal of a well-rounded, adaptable individual.

But I’m thinking even bigger. With The John Connor Project, I aim to:

  • Elevate IT and Cybersecurity Professionals to be as valued as electricians or mechanics, highlighting their role in defending digital frontiers.
  • Protect ‘Main Street’ from AI-Driven Threats by making cybersecurity expertise accessible to individuals and small businesses, not just big corporations.
  • Educate the Public on digital security, data privacy, and self-hosted AI solutions.
  • Connect People with Professionals through a directory that matches cybersecurity experts with those who need practical security solutions.

This isn’t just about building a career; it’s about building a movement—one that prioritizes digital sovereignty, practical security, and truth-seeking in an age where these values are more critical than ever.

Looking Ahead

My immediate focus is clear:

  • Complete BTL1 - SOC Analyst and CCNA certifications.
  • Secure a Cybersecurity Analyst role where I can contribute to defending critical assets.
  • Continue expanding Renaissance Technologist and The John Connor Project to create a ripple effect of knowledge, empowerment, and protection.
